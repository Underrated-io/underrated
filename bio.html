  1. Repeat to yourself: "I am good. I am great. I am worthy of human friendship"
  2. Read How to Make Friends and Influence People.
  3. Ask people questions about themselves.
  4. Don't be awkward
  5. Play canasta or pinochle with people. Join your Grandma's bridge club if you have to.
  6. Strike up a conversation with that cute boy/girl/non-zombie. What are you waiting for, another apocalypse?
